This is a small test post, mostly here so the layout has something to chew on. The point is not really the words. The point is spacing, rhythm, line length, and whether the page feels nice to sit inside.

I like blogs that feel quiet. Not empty exactly, but unhurried. A little bit of atmosphere, a little bit of texture, and then mostly words. The internet has enough cards and modals and carousels already.
